**Stage 1 Case Presentation:**

**Chief Complaint:**
A 45-year-old male presents to the emergency department with fever, cough, and night sweats.

**History of Present Illness:**
The patient reports that his symptoms began one month ago with fever, cough, and shortness of breath. He performed a home COVID-19 test, which was positive. As his symptoms worsened, he visited the emergency department and was diagnosed with community-acquired pneumonia. He was prescribed doxycycline and cephalexin for one week but did not experience improvement. His cough has since worsened and is now productive of non-bloody sputum. He also describes drenching night sweats. He denies any weight loss.

**Past Medical History:**
- Recently diagnosed diabetes mellitus (three months ago) after presenting with polyuria; initial HbA1c was 8.4%.
- Remote history of latent tuberculosis in the 1990s, treated with a 3-6 month course (exact duration uncertain).

**Medications:**
- Metformin 500mg twice daily (not currently taking).

**Family History:**
- Non-contributory.

**Social History:**
- Born in Mexico; moved to the United States in the 1990s.
- Works in a taqueria.
- Recent travel includes a trip to Lake Tahoe 1.5 months ago and a visit to family in Southern California one year ago.
- Denies tobacco and alcohol use.

**Allergies:**
- No known drug allergies.

**Review of Systems:**
- Negative except as mentioned in HPI.

**Vital Signs:**
- Temperature: 39.2°C (102.6°F)
- Heart rate: 95 beats per minute
- Blood pressure: 122/74mmHg
- Respiratory rate: 29 breaths per minute
- Oxygen saturation: 95% on room air

**Physical Examination:**
- **General:** Ill-appearing male in no acute distress.
- **Head, Eyes, Ears, Nose, Throat (HEENT):** Unremarkable.
- **Cardiovascular:** Tachycardia present; normal heart sounds without murmurs, rubs, or gallops; no jugular venous distention.
- **Respiratory:** Decreased breath sounds in the left upper lung field.
- **Abdomen:** Soft, non-tender, non-distended; normal bowel sounds.
- **Extremities/Skin:** No rashes or lesions; no erythema nodosum noted.
