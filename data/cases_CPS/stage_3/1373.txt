

**Case Presentation - Stage 3**

An 83-year-old woman presents with six weeks of fatigue, unintentional weight loss, and a dry cough. She denies fevers, chills, or night sweats.

Her medical history is significant for acute myeloid leukemia (AML) diagnosed 30 months ago, evolved from polycythemia vera. She was treated with the IDH2 inhibitor enasidenib for 24 months before a relapse and is currently on the FLT3 inhibitor gilteritinib. She was diagnosed with psoriasis two months ago.

She was born in Germany and has lived in the United States for the past 50 years. She does not use tobacco. Medications include cyclosporine and ustekinumab; she has had indeterminate interferon-gamma release assay (IGRA) results twice.

Allergies: None.

**Vital Signs:**

- Temperature: 99.7°F (37.6°C)
- Heart Rate: 87 beats per minute
- Blood Pressure: 117/82 mmHg
- Respiratory Rate: 15 breaths per minute
- Oxygen Saturation: 96% on room air

**Physical Examination:**

- **General:** Comfortable, no acute distress.
- **Cardiovascular:** Regular rate and rhythm; no murmurs, rubs, or gallops.
- **Pulmonary:** Crackles auscultated in the right lower lung field.
- **Abdominal:** Non-tender, non-distended.
- **Neurological:** Intact.
- **Extremities/Skin:** No edema; skin without notable lesions.

**Laboratory Results:**

- **Complete Blood Count (CBC):**
  - WBC: 5.2×10⁹/L
    - Neutrophils: 82%
    - Lymphocytes: 16%
    - Rare blasts present
  - Hemoglobin: 11.7g/dL
  - Platelets: 258×10³/µL

- **Comprehensive Metabolic Panel (CMP):**
  - Within normal limits
  - Creatinine: 0.8mg/dL
  - Total Bilirubin: 0.6mg/dL

- **Serum Tests:**
  - Urine Histoplasma antigen: Negative
  - Serum Blastomyces antigen: Negative
  - Serum Galactomannan: Negative
  - Serum 1,3-beta-D-glucan: Negative

**Imaging:**

- **Chest CT Scan:** Shows a lobulated mass in the right lower lobe measuring 3×3cm, previously noted at less than 1cm two years prior.

**Additional Diagnostic Studies:**

- **Sputum Analysis:** Patient unable to produce sputum.
- **Lung Biopsy:**
  - No evidence of malignancy.
  - Presence of necrotizing granulomas.
- **Acid-Fast Bacilli (AFB) Staining:** Positive.
- **PCR Testing for Mycobacterium tuberculosis:** Negative.
- **Culture of Lung Nodule:** pending

**Electrocardiogram (EKG):** Normal sinus rhythm.
