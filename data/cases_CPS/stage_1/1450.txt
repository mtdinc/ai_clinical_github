**Stage 1 Case Presentation:**

A 35-year-old woman with no significant past medical history presents to the emergency department with one week of nausea, vomiting, headache, and dizziness.

**History of Present Illness:**

The patient reports that over the past week, she has been experiencing dizziness, fatigue, and nausea accompanied by vomiting approximately three times per day. The emesis is non-bloody and occasionally bilious. She notes a significant decrease in oral intake of food and fluids. She denies any recent illness or sick contacts. She has not used nonsteroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs) or supplements. She has not experienced fevers, chills, vision changes, or significant diarrhea. She reports no changes in urination. She denies night sweats, joint pain, fatigue, or rashes. Over the past six months, she has intentionally lost 10-15 pounds through diet and exercise. She has never been pregnant.

She mentions that she has not seen a doctor in many years but recalls having a high blood pressure reading at a health fair last year. At baseline, she is fully functional and independent in all activities.

**Past Medical History:**

- Childhood tonsillectomy.

**Medications:**

- None.

**Allergies:**

- No known drug allergies.

**Family History:**

- Mother and grandmother have a history of hypertension.

**Social History:**

- Works in customer service with no known exposure to chemicals or toxins.
- Quit cigarette smoking one year ago.
- Denies alcohol and illicit drug use.
- No recent travel.
- No pets at home.

**Review of Systems:**

- Negative except as stated in the history of present illness.

**Physical Examination:**

- **Vital Signs:**
  - Temperature: Within normal limits.
  - Heart Rate: 99 beats per minute.
  - Blood Pressure: 211/156 mmHg.
  - Respiratory Rate: 20 breaths per minute.
  - Oxygen Saturation: 100% on room air.

- **General Appearance:**
  - Obese woman in no acute distress.

- **Head, Eyes, Ears, Nose, Throat (HEENT):**
  - Normocephalic, atraumatic.
  - Pupils equal, round, and reactive to light.
  - Extraocular movements intact.
  - Oral mucosa moist without lesions.

- **Cardiovascular:**
  - Regular rate and rhythm.
  - No murmurs, rubs, or gallops.
  - Peripheral pulses intact.

- **Pulmonary:**
  - Lungs clear to auscultation bilaterally.
  - No wheezes, rales, or rhonchi.

- **Abdominal:**
  - Soft, non-tender, non-distended.
  - No organomegaly.
  - Bowel sounds present.

- **Neurological:**
  - Alert and oriented to person, place, and time.
  - Cranial nerves II-XII intact.
  - Motor strength 5/5 in all extremities.

- **Skin/Extremities:**
  - Warm and dry.
