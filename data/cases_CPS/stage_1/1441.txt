**Stage 1 Case Presentation:**

A 42-year-old female presents to the emergency department with a 2-3 day history of fever, myalgias, and body aches. She reports that she initially visited her primary care physician, where she was diagnosed with a viral syndrome after negative influenza and COVID-19 tests. She was discharged with symptomatic treatment but has experienced no improvement. Her symptoms have since worsened, including increased myalgias, cough, and headache. She denies any visual changes or sick contacts.

- **Medications:**

- **Family History:**
