She attributes symptoms to recent increased work stress and poor sleep due to night sweats, which she assumes are perimenopausal symptoms.

- Migraine with aura
- Iron deficiency anemia (resolved)

- Recent use of preworkout supplements

**Social History:**

- Social drinker (1-2 glasses of wine nightly)

- Avid Peloton user
- Frequent international travel (returned from Tokyo 2 weeks ago)

**Family History:**
