**Stage 1: Initial Presentation**

A 23-year-old female presents to the ED with a 2-day history of intermittent lower back pain and mild diarrhea. She initially attributed the symptoms to her menstrual period, which started 3 days ago, and a new exercise routine she began last week at the gym. Today, she developed nausea and a loss of appetite.
