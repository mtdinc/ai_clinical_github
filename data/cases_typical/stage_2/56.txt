**Stage 1: Initial Presentation**

A 54-year-old male presents to the emergency department with dizziness and weakness that began this morning. He reports feeling lightheaded upon standing and had a near-fainting episode while getting out of bed. Over the past two days, he has experienced fatigue and notes that his wife mentioned he looked paler than usual. He denies chest pain, palpitations, shortness of breath, or recent illness. He has a history of chronic back pain and has been taking over-the-counter ibuprofen regularly for pain management. He also mentions occasional upper abdominal discomfort but attributes it to indigestion or stress.
